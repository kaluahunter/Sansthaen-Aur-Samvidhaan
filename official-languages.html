Official Languages and Transitional Provisions

Description

This section deals with the linguistic framework of the country, as well as temporary and transitional provisions made during the early years of the Constitution. It also includes the short title, commencement, and formal repeal provisions.

Part XVII: Official Language

Defines Hindi as the official language, and provisions for the use of English and other regional languages.

Part XXI: Temporary, Transitional, and Special Provisions

Includes temporary provisions, such as the now-repealed Article 370 for Jammu and Kashmir, and provisions for other states.

Part XXII: Short Title, Commencement, Authoritative Text in Hindi, and Repeals
