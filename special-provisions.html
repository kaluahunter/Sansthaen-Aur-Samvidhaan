Special Provisions for Certain Regions and Classes

Description

This category provides special protections and governance for historically marginalized or unique regions and communities, including Scheduled and Tribal areas, and provisions for backward classes (SCs, STs, OBCs).

Part X: Scheduled and Tribal Areas

Provides for the administration and control of areas with significant tribal populations, safeguarding their rights.

Part XVI: Special Provisions Relating to Certain Classes

Establishes measures to protect disadvantaged groups (SCs, STs, OBCs), including reservations in education, jobs, and legislatures.
