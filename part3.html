Part 4 - The Constitution of India

12. Definition

The term "fundamental rights" includes a range of essential rights that safeguard individual freedoms, including the right to equality, freedom of speech, and protection of life and liberty.

13. Laws inconsistent with or in derogation of the fundamental rights

Any law that conflicts with or undermines fundamental rights can be reviewed by the judiciary and may be declared void if found inconsistent with the Constitution.
